Inside the judgment engine

How the AI decides who matters today

Every Why-Now signal is sourced and weighted. You can audit it, dispute it, and the system learns from your verdict.

Signals we watch
  • LinkedIn Job changes, posts, role announcements, company moves
  • Gmail Reply patterns, thread silence, time-since-last-touch
  • Calendar Meetings held, no-shows, recurring touchpoints
  • News Funding rounds, hires, press, podcast appearances
  • Cadence Drift from your custom frequency for each tier
  • Reciprocity Open favors, intros given, intros owed
How we weigh them
  • Timing windows decay fast — a job post 2 days old beats 7 days silent
  • Reciprocity trumps cadence — if they helped you, you reach out within 14 days
  • Counter-signals rule contacts out — PTO, just-funded inbox overload, peer noise
  • Confidence shown per card — high / medium / low. We don't fake certainty.
  • Your verdict teaches the model — thumbs-down a signal and it weighs less for you
